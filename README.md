This is a Raycast extension for [Vesslo](https://vesslo.com), allowing you to search your apps and check for updates directly from Raycast.

## Features

- **Search Apps**: Quickly find your apps by name, developer, tags, or memo.
- **View Updates**: See all available updates for your apps (Homebrew, Sparkle, App Store, Manual).
- **Bulk Update**: Update all Homebrew apps at once.
- **Browse by Tag**: View apps grouped by your Vesslo tags.
- **Deep Integration**: Open apps in Vesslo, view in Finder, or launch directly.

## Prerequisites

- **Vesslo App**: You need to have the Vesslo app installed and running on your Mac.
- **Data Export**: The extension reads data from `~/.vesslo/data.json`, which is automatically generated by Vesslo.
